# VervoerManager – Glossary

## Domain Terms

| Term | Dutch | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Company** | Bedrijf | Employer. Owns drivers, vehicles |
| **Client** | Klant | Customer company. Receives drivers/rides |
| **Driver** | Chauffeur | Employee who drives |
| **Car** | Vrachtwagen | Truck/vehicle |
| **Ride** | Rit | Planned trip |
| **PartRide** | Deelrit | Partial ride with approval flow |
| **Contact Person** | Contactpersoon | Client admin/planner |
| **Charter** | Charters | Long-term vehicle charter |

## HR & Contract Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **CAO** | Collectieve Arbeidsovereenkomst – Collective labor agreement |
| **ZZP** | Zelfstandige Zonder Personeel – Self-employed/freelancer |
| **Inleen** | Secondment (driver lent to another company) |
| **Brief Loonschaal** | Simple wage scale (non-CAO) |
| **Deeltijd** | Part-time |
| **VOG** | Verklaring Omtrent Gedrag – Certificate of conduct |
| **ADV** | Compensation hours days |
| **TvT** | Tijd voor Tijd – Time-for-time (overtime) |

## Finance & Rates

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Verkoop** | Sales rate (what we charge client) |
| **Inkoop** | Purchase rate (what we pay driver) |
| **Offerte** | Quote/quotation |
| **Jaaropgave** | Annual statement |
| **UVS** | Payroll/tax reporting |

## Vehicle & Documents

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **APK** | Algemene Periodieke Keuring – Vehicle inspection (MOT) |
| **NIWO** | Diesel fuel certification |
| **Kiwa** | Certification body |
| **Reglement** | Internal regulations / policy documents |
